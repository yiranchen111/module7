What is Glaucoma?

Glaucoma is a group of eye conditions that damage the optic nerve, vital for good vision, often caused by abnormally high pressure in your eye.

Recognizing Glaucoma

Symptoms include severe eye pain, nausea, red eyes, blurred vision, and halos around lights. Early detection is crucial.

Self-Care for Glaucoma

Maintaining regular eye exams, using prescribed eye drops, and protecting your eyes from injury are essential steps.
